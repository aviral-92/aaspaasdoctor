we have created this site aaspaasdoctor.com because in case of emergency our first question in hindi is "koi doctor hai aaspaas" means is their any doctor available near by. Almost we all have encountered this situation in our life. This situation get worst in rural areas of our country. Our platform aaspaasdoctor.com will connect each individual to nearby medical facilities and it will save many life's as well as time of many person. Another problem is when we take appointment with doctor we have to physically visit their if doctor is not available then our time and efforts are gone waste . To solve all this kind of problem we have come up with platform aaspaasdoctor.com to solve these problems..
